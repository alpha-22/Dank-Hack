Just a Little Hard Work

For Wannabe Fit

  • A gym beginner plan should include basic fitness equipment such as cardio machines, dumbbells, and resistance bands.
  • Personal training can be beneficial for beginners to learn proper form and technique and create a workout plan that matches their fitness level and goals.
  • Group classes, such as yoga or Pilates, can add variety to a beginner's workout routine and provide social support and guidance from a professional instructor.
Your Sincere Efforts

For Wannabe Jacked

  • An intermediate gym plan should include access to more advanced equipment such as squat racks, cable machines, and leg press machines.
  • Personal training is still beneficial for intermediate gym-goers to help them progress and achieve their fitness goals.
  • Intermediate gym-goers can benefit from more intense group classes such as spin or HIIT classes that offer a challenging workout that pushes individuals out of their comfort zone.
Hustle

For Aspiring BodyBuilders

  • An advanced gym plan should include access to specialized equipment such as Olympic weightlifting platforms, power racks, and specialty machines.
  • Personal training should be personalized and tailored to the individual's specific goals, including powerlifting, bodybuilding, or sports performance.
  • Advanced gym-goers can benefit from high-intensity group classes such as boot camp or cross-training classes that push individuals to their limits and provide a competitive and challenging workout environment.
